About <Clockwork> US-Sargeras

Summary

<Clockwork> is a progression-oriented raiding guild on US-Sargeras. We started as a group of casual raiders in Cataclysm, but some of us have been playing together since TBC. <Clockwork> strives to efficiently raid the newest content on a light schedule of only 8 hours a week. We have a laid-back atmosphere in raiding, but that doesn't mean we don't take progression seriously. We pride ourselves on learning bosses quickly and killing them in a reasonable amount of pulls. We are always respectful of each other and have zero tolerance for drama. We all play for fun and are always seeking new players with a similar mindset.

Schedule

We raid two nights per week, four hours a night as part of our mandatory raiding schedule. We expect very good attendance from our raiders (90%+) and at least some notice if you will be absent. We also do normal or alt runs on other days of the week when people are free for fun.

Thursday/Sunday 19:30-23:30 CST

Interested? Here's what we are looking for in an applicant:

  • 675+ Item Level
  • Knowledge of your class
  • Fast learner
  • Mumble and a working microphone
  • Similar experience and gear on an alt is a plus
  • Willing to sit out on fights occasionally
